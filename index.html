About The Society

The Blessed Star Society focuses on mythology, religion, worship and magic. We bring forgotten topics to light and study various spiritual traditions and entities across cultures. Our purpose is educational and philosophical: exploring the symbolism, history and modern meaning of magical and religious traditions.

Founder & High Priest

Mohit is a 13 year old fond of the occult who studies mythology and symbolism. He reads tarot and oracle cards and is deeply interested in spiritual traditions, philosophy and mystical symbolism.

He founded The Blessed Star Society for practitioners and scholars who study the occult. The society focuses on magic in theory and its modern meaning while encouraging respectful research into spiritual traditions.

Priest / Priestess Initiate Course

A Priest or Priestess refers to someone who practices occult philosophy and serves a community or deity through study, symbolism and guidance.

The Priest/Priestess Initiate Course contains magical workshops and the study of light magic in theory. It brings light to topics of the occult and explores their deeper symbolism and philosophical meaning.

Students are introduced to modern forms of worship and may specialise in the mythology and symbolism of a chosen deity or spiritual tradition.
